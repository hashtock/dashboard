Hashtock

Hashtock is a simple game.

In short: you can buy and sell virtual shares of Hashtag, as on a stock market.

You can do that either with bank. Then price is set based on popularity of that hashtag on Twitter

Alternatively you can trade with other users. Then you can set price or accept price set by others.
